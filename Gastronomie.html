Gastronomy and our good old town of Paris

let’s start with a bit of history

If the French gastronomic meal is classified since 2010 in the heritage unesco World Intangible Cultural Heritage, Paris is the best place to taste it! The city is also revealed through a cuisine that is more modern, open, creative, innovative, inspired the Fooding® movement. From the rise of street-food to the enthusiasm for multidisciplinary restaurants, trends Today, there are many ways of living in the capital. The culinary offer, in constant renewal, remains both cutting-edge and innovative. Dinner in a mini-restaurant or grocery store, watching the chefs cooking, letting themselves be guided by a single menu
