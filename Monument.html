Welcome To Paris

The Eiffel Tower is the symbol of Paris and more widely of France. It was built by Gustave Eiffel for the Universal Exhibition of 1889. It is one of the most visited monuments in the world. From the discovery of the historic gardens to the dazzling view of Paris from the top, discovering the Eiffel Tower is an incredible experience. On the first floor, on the glass floor, you feel the unique sensation of walking above the void. On the platform, along the outside walkway, screens, tactile tiles, showcases, digital albums, reproductions of objects, etc., allow visitors to learn about all the often unknown aspects of the Tower through a museographic tour.
